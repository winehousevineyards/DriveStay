    Terms of Service & How It Works

    Last updated: June 2026

    How Driveway Host Works

    Driveway Host is a peer-to-peer marketplace connecting property owners ("Hosts") who have spare driveway or parking space with travelers and commuters ("Guests") seeking convenient, affordable parking.

    We charge a 10% service fee, a 4% guest protection fee, and a 3% host protection fee on each booking subtotal. Insurance is mandatory on all bookings and covers guest trip liability and host property damage. Hosts receive their payout via Stripe Connect within 24 hours of guest check-in.

    Discount Structure

    • Weekly discount (20%): Automatically applied when stay is 7 or more nights.
    • Monthly discount (40%): Automatically applied when stay is 28 or more nights.
    • Discounts apply to the base nightly rate before fees.

    Guest Identity Verification

    All Guests must complete a three-step identity verification before their first booking:

    • Step 1 — Driver's License: Upload a photo of your valid government-issued ID.
    • Step 2 — Vehicle Info: Provide your VIN (17 characters, no I/O/Q) and license plate.
    • Step 3 — Insurance: Confirm your vehicle insurance provider, policy number, and expiry.

    Host Requirements

    • Hosts must carry valid short-term rental property insurance (e.g., via Thimble).
    • Listings must accurately represent the space, access, and limitations.
    • New listings are reviewed within 48 hours before going live.

    Cancellation Policy

    • 48+ hours before check-in: Full refund minus broker fee.
    • 24–48 hours before: 50% refund.
    • Less than 24 hours: No refund.

    Liability

    Driveway Host is a marketplace platform and is not liable for property damage, theft, or injury occurring on a listed space. Use of the platform constitutes acceptance of these terms.

    Terms of Service

    Last updated: June 2026

    1. Acceptance of Terms

    By accessing or using Driveway Host, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. Driveway Host is a peer-to-peer marketplace that connects hosts offering parking spaces and driveways with guests seeking temporary parking. Driveway Host acts as a neutral platform and is not a party to any rental agreement between hosts and guests.

    2. Eligibility

    You must be at least 18 years old to use Driveway Host. By using the platform, you represent that you have the legal authority to enter into binding agreements.

    3. Host Responsibilities

    Hosts are responsible for accurately describing their space, ensuring the space is safe and accessible during the booked period, complying with local laws and HOA rules regarding parking rentals, and having appropriate insurance coverage. Driveway Host is not liable for property damage or incidents during a booking.

    4. Guest Responsibilities

    Guests must only use the space for the purposes described in the listing, respect the host's property and rules, not leave vehicles beyond the checkout time, and be reachable during the booking period.

    5. Payments and Fees

    Driveway Host charges a 12% service fee to guests and a 3% processing fee to hosts. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Hosts receive payouts within 24 hours of guest check-in via Stripe Connect.

    6. Cancellation Policy

    Full refund for cancellations made 48+ hours before check-in. 50% refund for cancellations 24–48 hours before check-in. No refund for cancellations under 24 hours before check-in. Driveway Host service fees are non-refundable.

    7. Prohibited Uses

    You may not use Driveway Host for storing vehicles long-term without host consent, commercial vehicle operations, illegal activities, or circumventing the platform to pay hosts directly.

    8. Limitation of Liability

    Driveway Host is provided "as is." We are not liable for property damage, theft, personal injury, or disputes between hosts and guests. Our maximum liability is limited to the transaction amount. Disputes should be filed within 24 hours of checkout via our in-app messaging system.

    9. Contact

    Questions about these terms? Email us at drivewayhost@pm.me

    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: June 2026

    1. Information We Collect

    We collect information you provide directly: name, email, phone number, government ID (for identity verification), vehicle information, and payment details. We also collect usage data including listings viewed, bookings made, and messages sent.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to facilitate bookings between hosts and guests, process payments via Stripe, send transactional emails (booking confirmations, receipts, messages), verify identity, improve the platform, and comply with legal obligations.

    3. Information Sharing

    We share limited information with: Stripe (payment processing), hosts/guests as needed to complete a booking (first name and vehicle info only), and law enforcement when required by law. We do not sell your personal information.

    4. Data Security

    All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and never stored on Driveway Host servers. We use Supabase with row-level security policies to protect your data.

    5. Your Rights (CCPA/GDPR)

    You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email drivewayhost@pm.me. We will respond within 30 days. California residents have additional rights under CCPA including the right to know what data we collect and the right to opt out of data sale (we do not sell data).

    6. Cookies

    We use only essential cookies required for authentication (Supabase session cookies). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

    7. Data Retention

    Account data is retained until you delete your account. Booking records are retained for 7 years for tax/legal compliance. You may request account deletion at any time.

    Privacy questions: drivewayhost@pm.me

    How Can We Help?

    Find answers to common questions about booking, hosting, payments, and more.

    Booking
    How do I book a parking space?
    Browse available spaces on the Browse page. Click a listing to view details, select your dates and times, then click "Book Now." You'll be prompted to sign in (or create a free account) and then pay securely via Stripe. You'll receive a booking confirmation by email with the host's address and access instructions.
    What is Instant Book?
    Instant Book listings are confirmed immediately — no need to wait for host approval. These spaces display a ⚡ bolt icon in search results. You can filter for Instant Book spaces using the filter chips on the Browse page.
    Can I book a space for multiple nights?
    Yes. Most listings support multi-night bookings. Use the date picker in the booking form to select your check-in and check-out dates. Many hosts also offer discounted monthly rates, which you'll see listed on their space's detail page.
    How do I access the space after booking?
    After booking, you'll receive the host's exact address, any gate codes or access instructions, and the host's contact info through our in-app messaging system. If you have trouble finding or accessing the space, message the host directly through your dashboard.
    What happens if the space isn't available when I arrive?
    First, contact the host through your dashboard. If the issue can't be resolved, contact our 24/7 support team. In verified cases where the space is unusable, we'll issue a full refund and help you find an alternative nearby.
    Hosting
    How do I list my driveway?
    Click "List Your Space" in the navigation. Sign in or create an account, then fill out the listing form with your space details — size, amenities, availability, and pricing. Once submitted, your listing goes into a quick review process and typically goes live within a few hours.
    How much should I charge?
    Rates depend on your location, amenities, and proximity to events. Standard driveways average $25–$55/night. Spaces near stadiums or in cities can command $60–$150+ on event nights. We recommend starting at the lower end of your market and adjusting based on demand. Use the Browse page to see what similar spaces near you charge.
    When do I get paid?
    Hosts receive payouts via Stripe 24 hours after the guest's check-in time. This delay protects against last-minute cancellations. Payouts go directly to your connected bank account and typically clear within 1–3 business days depending on your bank.
    What is the host service fee?
    Driveway Host charges a 10% service fee on each booking, deducted from your payout. For example, if your space is listed at $40/night and a guest books for 2 nights ($80 total), you receive $72. Guests also pay a small booking fee on their end to help cover platform costs.
    Can I decline a booking request?
    Yes. If your listing is not set to Instant Book, you can review and decline booking requests within 24 hours. For Instant Book listings, guests can book immediately, but you can still cancel a booking with appropriate notice if there's a conflict. Frequent cancellations may affect your host standing.
    Payments
    What payment methods are accepted?
    We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), Apple Pay, and Google Pay through our Stripe integration. Payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers.
    Is my payment information secure?
    Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. Driveway Host never sees or stores your full card number — only the last 4 digits for display purposes. Stripe's encryption and fraud detection protect every transaction.
    When is my card charged?
    Your card is charged immediately when you complete a booking. For Instant Book listings, the full amount is charged at the time of booking. For request-to-book listings, a hold is placed on your card when you submit the request, and charged only if the host accepts.
    Cancellations & Refunds
    What is the cancellation policy?
    The default policy is: Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before check-in. 50% refund if cancelled 24–48 hours before check-in. No refund for cancellations within 24 hours of check-in. Individual hosts may set stricter or more flexible policies — check the listing's detail page before booking.
    How do I cancel a booking?
    Go to your Dashboard → Bookings, find the booking you want to cancel, and click "Cancel." The system will show your refund amount based on the applicable policy. Refunds typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days.
    What if the host cancels on me?
    If a host cancels your confirmed booking, you'll receive a full refund automatically. We'll also apply a platform credit toward your next booking as compensation. Hosts who cancel confirmed bookings receive a warning and may be suspended from the platform after repeated violations.
    Safety & Trust
    Are hosts and guests verified?
    Yes. All users must verify their email address. Guests must also verify their identity with a government-issued ID before their first booking. Hosts with Verified badges have completed additional identity and property verification through our trust & safety process.
    What does Host Protection cover?
    Our Host Protection program covers property damage caused by guests during a booking, up to $10,000 per incident. To file a claim, document the damage with photos, submit a report through your dashboard within 48 hours of check-out, and our team will review it within 3–5 business days.
    What if I have a safety emergency during a booking?
    Always call 911 first for any immediate safety emergency. Then contact our 24/7 trust & safety support team via email at drivewayhost@pm.me. For non-emergency disputes or concerns, use the Dispute feature in your dashboard.
    Account
    How do I create an account?
    Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation. Enter your email and a password, choose whether you're joining as a guest or host (you can do both), and verify your email address. Your account is ready immediately.
    Can I be both a host and a guest?
    Absolutely. One account handles both. Your dashboard has separate sections for your bookings (as a guest) and your listings and host bookings. Many of our most active users both rent out their own driveway and book spaces in other cities when they travel.
    How do I delete my account?
    To request account deletion, email us at drivewayhost@pm.me with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." Please note that we're required to retain certain transaction records for legal and tax purposes for up to 7 years, but your personal profile will be removed.
