    Terms of Service & How It Works

    Last updated: June 2026

    How DriveStay Works

    DriveStay is a peer-to-peer marketplace connecting property owners ("Hosts") who have spare driveway or parking space with travelers and commuters ("Guests") seeking convenient, affordable parking.

    We charge a 12% broker fee and a 3% guest insurance fee on each booking subtotal. Hosts receive their payout via Stripe Connect within 24 hours of guest check-in.

    Discount Structure

    • Weekly discount (20%): Automatically applied when stay is 7 or more nights.
    • Monthly discount (40%): Automatically applied when stay is 28 or more nights.
    • Discounts apply to the base nightly rate before fees.

    Guest Identity Verification

    All Guests must complete a three-step identity verification before their first booking:

    • Step 1 — Driver's License: Upload a photo of your valid government-issued ID.
    • Step 2 — Vehicle Info: Provide your VIN (17 characters, no I/O/Q) and license plate.
    • Step 3 — Insurance: Confirm your vehicle insurance provider, policy number, and expiry.

    Host Requirements

    • Hosts must carry valid short-term rental property insurance (e.g., via Thimble).
    • Listings must accurately represent the space, access, and limitations.
    • New listings are reviewed within 48 hours before going live.

    Cancellation Policy

    • 48+ hours before check-in: Full refund minus broker fee.
    • 24–48 hours before: 50% refund.
    • Less than 24 hours: No refund.

    Liability

    DriveStay is a marketplace platform and is not liable for property damage, theft, or injury occurring on a listed space. Use of the platform constitutes acceptance of these terms.

    Terms of Service

    Last updated: June 2026

    1. Acceptance of Terms

    By accessing or using DriveStay, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. DriveStay is a peer-to-peer marketplace that connects hosts offering parking spaces and driveways with guests seeking temporary parking. DriveStay acts as a neutral platform and is not a party to any rental agreement between hosts and guests.

    2. Eligibility

    You must be at least 18 years old to use DriveStay. By using the platform, you represent that you have the legal authority to enter into binding agreements.

    3. Host Responsibilities

    Hosts are responsible for accurately describing their space, ensuring the space is safe and accessible during the booked period, complying with local laws and HOA rules regarding parking rentals, and having appropriate insurance coverage. DriveStay is not liable for property damage or incidents during a booking.

    4. Guest Responsibilities

    Guests must only use the space for the purposes described in the listing, respect the host's property and rules, not leave vehicles beyond the checkout time, and be reachable during the booking period.

    5. Payments and Fees

    DriveStay charges a 12% service fee to guests and a 3% processing fee to hosts. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Hosts receive payouts within 24 hours of guest check-in via Stripe Connect.

    6. Cancellation Policy

    Full refund for cancellations made 48+ hours before check-in. 50% refund for cancellations 24–48 hours before check-in. No refund for cancellations under 24 hours before check-in. DriveStay service fees are non-refundable.

    7. Prohibited Uses

    You may not use DriveStay for storing vehicles long-term without host consent, commercial vehicle operations, illegal activities, or circumventing the platform to pay hosts directly.

    8. Limitation of Liability

    DriveStay is provided "as is." We are not liable for property damage, theft, personal injury, or disputes between hosts and guests. Our maximum liability is limited to the transaction amount. Disputes should be filed within 24 hours of checkout via our in-app messaging system.

    9. Contact

    Questions about these terms? Email us at legal@drivestay.com

    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: June 2026

    1. Information We Collect

    We collect information you provide directly: name, email, phone number, government ID (for identity verification), vehicle information, and payment details. We also collect usage data including listings viewed, bookings made, and messages sent.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to facilitate bookings between hosts and guests, process payments via Stripe, send transactional emails (booking confirmations, receipts, messages), verify identity, improve the platform, and comply with legal obligations.

    3. Information Sharing

    We share limited information with: Stripe (payment processing), hosts/guests as needed to complete a booking (first name and vehicle info only), and law enforcement when required by law. We do not sell your personal information.

    4. Data Security

    All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and never stored on DriveStay servers. We use Supabase with row-level security policies to protect your data.

    5. Your Rights (CCPA/GDPR)

    You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email privacy@drivestay.com. We will respond within 30 days. California residents have additional rights under CCPA including the right to know what data we collect and the right to opt out of data sale (we do not sell data).

    6. Cookies

    We use only essential cookies required for authentication (Supabase session cookies). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

    7. Data Retention

    Account data is retained until you delete your account. Booking records are retained for 7 years for tax/legal compliance. You may request account deletion at any time.

    Privacy questions: privacy@drivestay.com
